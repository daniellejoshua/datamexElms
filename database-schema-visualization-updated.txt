🎯 IMPROVED ACADEMIC FLOW:
=========================
1. Admin creates SECTIONS for Program + Year Level (e.g., "BSIT-1A")
2. Assigns multiple SUBJECTS to that section through SECTION_SUBJECTS
3. Assigns TEACHERS to each subject within the section
4. Creates CLASS_SCHEDULES for each subject with specific times
5. Students enroll in SECTIONS and automatically get all subjects

✅ Benefits:
- Sections represent real class groups (BSIT-1A, BSCS-2B)
- Multiple subjects per section (Math, English, Programming, etc.)
- Individual teacher assignments per subject
- Flexible scheduling per subject within section
- Realistic academic structure

🚮 REMOVED TABLES:
==================
- COURSES (confusion between program and subject resolved)
- TEACHER_ASSIGNMENTS (replaced by teacher_id in SECTION_SUBJECTS)

📋 KEY RELATIONSHIPS:
====================
Programs (1) → (M) Sections
Sections (M) ← → (M) Subjects (via SECTION_SUBJECTS pivot)
Teachers (1) → (M) Section_Subjects
Section_Subjects (1) → (M) Class_Schedules
Students (M) → (M) Sections (via STUDENT_ENROLLMENTS)
